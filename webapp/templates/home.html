What is WeatherIt?

WeatherIt allows users to search for items that they are interested in marketing. The system then uses Google search data and past weather data to plot useful graphs that can show users how the interest in an item varies over time and how linked this is to the weather. With access to this information, users will be better informed and will be able to market different products at different times of the year to better reach potential customers and improve profits.
